About Us

More Than a Sports Company

Icy Sports was founded with a simple vision: make winter sports and ice hockey accessible, exciting, and sustainable in India.

What started as a passion for the sport has evolved into a platform connecting athletes, schools, government bodies, international organizations, and corporate partners to accelerate the growth of ice hockey nationwide.

Today, Icy Sports works across athlete development, events, leagues, coaching programs, grassroots outreach, and international collaborations.
